Junkbot Wiki

Welcome to the Junkbot Wiki!

The Junkbot avatar was released by Roblox in November 14, 2019. The Junkbot bundle became popular around the year 2020 when Roblox youtuber Flamingo leaded and started the Junkbot Raid with 220,000+ Junkbots attending the raid. Junkbot Raid Week had about 600,000+ more or less Junkbots participating in Da Hood, Life in Paradise, Event game, and a whole other bunch of different games making it one of the most biggest raid to have ever exist in Roblox history.

The official Junkbot bundle link is: https://www.roblox.com/bundles/589/Junkbot. The iconic part of the Junkbot is the head and color.

Link to official fandom: https://junkbot.fandom.com/wiki/Junkbot

Junkbot Wiki lists all real events that happened in Junkbot history in Roblox.

Junkbot Cult

The Junkbot Cult is one of the oldest Junkbot Factions to this day. Junkbot Cult started in November 9th, 2020 when the Great Junkbot Raid happened and the Golden Age of Junkbots came.Junkbot Cult is the 1st most powerful Junkbot faction, one of the oldest, and the central hub for Junkbots with the most members. Today there are 2 different Junkbot Cults but Junkbot Cult III is being debated on whether its a fan group or should be a official branch of Junkbot Cult.

The Junkbot Cult III Empire

ALSO KNOWN AS JUNKBOT CULT III AND THE REDCLIFF EMPIRE OFFICIALLY

The Junkbot Cult III Empire is a Junkbot Faction that the most famous and powerful Junkbot Faction in today's Junkbots world. The Junkbot Cult III Empire is a Junkbot Faction that was founded in 12/12/2023 founded by Junkbot Smith Family (JSF created in 4/7/2023) by Dmj[1] and Junkbot Cult III Empire has been in many wars throughout its history such as The BFR war, Noobian war, Birdian War, Ashleyan Empire War, and the most famous war The Bob Mob vs Junkbot War that all resulted in Junkbot Cult III Empire Victory.

The origin behind the name "Junkbot Cult III[2]" is because dmj is a fan of Junkbot Cult and Junkbot Cult II

They have their own website - www.numberarc.com which makes The Junkbot Cult III Empire a bit independent from Roblox

United Junkbots Nations (Officially Disbanded)

The United Junkbot Nations is a United Nations but specifically for Junkbots in Roblox. The UJN is a peacekeeper group that archives and documents the history of the Junkbot Community very much like UGoN (United Groups of Nations) [1] . The UJN has been in multiple wars against other Junkbot Factions such as Junkgod wars, Battle of Genoa, and others. 40+ Junkbot Factions were in the UJN but the UJN is officially dismembered.

More information about the UJN is in their official wiki that has been abandoned and partially nuked by Bob Mobs. R.I.P. UJN
